Commute Itinerary Feature Demo

Dwelligence calculates real transit commute times for every listing using OpenTripPlanner. When you set a commute filter, each listing's detail view shows a step-by-step itinerary with walking legs, subway rides, and total travel time.

STEP 1

Filtered Listing View COMMUTE ACTIVE

After applying a commute filter (L train, 14 St-Union Sq, within 20 min walk), the listing view shows only apartments that match. The Filters 1 badge indicates an active filter. Map pins cluster near L train stops in Brooklyn and Manhattan.

STEP 2

Commute Filter Configuration

The commute filter dropdown lets you pick a subway line, select specific stops, set a max walk time (1-20 min), and choose walk or bike mode. All NYC subway lines are available with their official MTA colors. You can also filter by station name, street address, or park.

STEP 3

Commute Itinerary Timeline

Each listing's detail modal includes a Commute section with a vertical timeline showing the complete door-to-door journey. This example shows a route from a Williamsburg apartment to 14 St-Union Sq: an 18-minute walk to Montrose Av station, an 11-minute L train ride, and a 4-minute walk to the destination. The total travel time (33 min) is prominently displayed.

STEP 4

Full Detail Modal in Context

The commute itinerary sits alongside listing details like price, bedrooms, year built, a location map, reaction buttons (Dislike / I Would Live There / Like), and the source link. This gives renters everything they need to evaluate a listing's commute viability at a glance.
